Italy

Description

The myth that Italy was built upon was that, two brothers named Romulus and Remus who are descendants of Aeneas founded Rome (the capital) on April 21 753 BC.

Reasons why I want to go to Italy

Italy has many different landmarks, one of the most popular being the Colosseum!

It can seat between 50,000-80,000 people, where they would have gladiator contests and more in the realm of that.

I find it interesting to go to a place that was made in 70 AD and to find out how/why it became so popular.
